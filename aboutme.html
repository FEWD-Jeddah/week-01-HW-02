All About Giraff

Giraff Apearance

Giraffes are gentle creatures with gracefully long necks. This gives them the ability to reach opportunities that are not available to others.

Giraff Symbolism

the giraffe symbolizes grace, peace, individuality, protection, communication, perception, and farsightedness.

Giraff Spots and Honrs

  • A giraffe's spots are much like human fingerprints.
  • N o two individual giraffes have exactly the same pattern.
  • Both male and female giraffes have two distinct, hair-covered horns called ossicones.
  • Male giraffes use their horns to sometimes fight with other males.
